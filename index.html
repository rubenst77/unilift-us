The UNILIFT Hoist

One hoist.
Unlimited height.

An electric wire-rope traction hoist for man-riding and material lifting. Its α-shaped rope path lets the rope pass through the machine, so lift height is limited only by the rope you deploy.

  • Man-riding certified Designed and certified to EN 1808 for lifting people, with safety devices built in.
  • 1,500 to 2,200 lbs WLL Three models, one platform. Single or three-phase, 208 V / 60 Hz.
  • Integrated OSLA brake Independent overspeed safety lock, factory-fitted. The hoist cannot run without it.

Build quality

Engineered and labelled in Luxembourg

Every UNILIFT is factory-tested before it ships. The control box integrates E-stop, up/down, overload alarm and status indicators in one sealed IP55 unit.

Safety mechanism

The OSLA overspeed lock, up close

Mounted above the rope inlet, the OSLA secondary brake grips the rope on breakage or overspeed. It is mechanical and independent, needing no electrical power to arrest a fall.

Serviceability

Built to be connected and serviced

Industrial 3-pin (1P) or 4-pin (3P) connectors, an internal hour-meter, and a 500-hour overhaul cycle make integration and maintenance straightforward.

Standard Safety

Hoist main features.

All required safety devices integrated as standard, per EN 1808.

Built-in Overload Mechanism

When the load reaches or exceeds the limit, the hoist stops rising and will only descend. Trips at 125% WLL.

Safety Lowering Mechanism

In a power failure, the electro-mechanical brake can be released manually for a safe, controlled descent.

Hour Counter

An internal running-hours counter in the connection box records operating time, so service intervals are always known.

Attachment Points

Suspend from a central load pin, or fix at the housing corners with a hook, screw or pins. Adaptable to any rig.
