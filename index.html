The UNILIFT Hoist

One hoist.
Unlimited height.

An electric wire-rope traction hoist for man-riding and material lifting. Its α-shaped rope path lets the rope pass through the machine, so lift height is limited only by the rope you deploy.

  • Man-riding certified Designed and certified to EN 1808 for lifting people, with safety devices built in.
  • 1,500 to 2,200 lbs WLL Three models, one platform. Single or three-phase, 208 V / 60 Hz.
  • Integrated OSLA brake Independent overspeed safety lock, factory-fitted. The hoist cannot run without it.

Build quality

Engineered and labelled in Luxembourg

Every UNILIFT is factory-tested before it ships. The control box integrates E-stop, up/down, overload alarm and status indicators in one sealed IP55 unit.

Safety mechanism

The OSLA overspeed lock, up close

Mounted above the rope inlet, the OSLA secondary brake grips the rope on breakage or overspeed. It is mechanical and independent, needing no electrical power to arrest a fall.

Serviceability

Built to be connected and serviced

Industrial 3-pin (1P) or 4-pin (3P) connectors, an internal hour-meter, and a 500-hour overhaul cycle make integration and maintenance straightforward.

Standard Safety

Hoist main features.

All required safety devices integrated as standard, per EN 1808.

Built-in Overload Mechanism

When the load reaches or exceeds the limit, the hoist stops rising and will only descend. Trips at 125% WLL.

Safety Lowering Mechanism

In a power failure, the electro-mechanical brake can be released manually for a safe, controlled descent.

Hour Counter

An internal running-hours counter in the connection box records operating time, so service intervals are always known.

Attachment Points

Suspend from a central load pin, or fix at the housing corners with a hook, screw or pins. Adaptable to any rig.

FAQ

UNILIFT (12 questions)

Certification & US market

Yes. The UNILIFT is UL 1323 listed and CSA Z271 certified, and manufactured to EN 1808:2015 and EN 60204-1. These are the recognized standards for powered access and suspended-platform hoists. Full certificates are available on request.
Yes. The UNILIFT is designed and certified to EN 1808 for man-riding, lifting people, not just materials, with the required safety devices built in as standard: overload protection, dual braking, the independent OSLA overspeed lock, and a manual lowering mechanism.
OSHA compliance for suspended and powered-platform work is site-based: it depends on the employer's rigging, anchorage, inspections, and an independent personal fall-arrest system for each worker, per OSHA 1926/1910. The UNILIFT is built and certified (UL 1323, CSA Z271, EN 1808) to be the compliant hoist within that setup. We supply the documentation, load ratings, and inspection guidance your competent person needs to keep the installation OSHA-ready. For your specific application, talk to our engineers.

Specification & fit

Power supply and motor. The UNI70A-9P11-S runs 2 hp on 208 V single-phase (12 A), ideal where only single-phase power is available. The UNI70A-9P11-T and UNI100A-9P11-T run 3 hp on 208 V three-phase (9 A). Capacity and speed are otherwise identical between the two UNI70A models; the UNI100A steps capacity up to 2,200 lbs.
Effectively unlimited. The wire rope passes straight through the hoist instead of spooling onto a drum, so the machine size never limits your height. Only the length of rope you deploy does. Reels are available from 328 to 1,312 ft.
Each unit is 159 lbs (72 kg), 11.8 in wide by 30.2 in tall (300 by 767 mm), compact enough to be handled and positioned by a small crew, with a die-cast aluminium housing rated IP55 for exposed sites.

Safety

The integrated OSLA secondary brake locks onto the rope automatically and arrests the descent. It's an independent mechanical device that needs no electrical power to work, and the hoist is not permitted to operate without it. This is the core fall-protection feature for man-riding use.
A manual lowering mechanism lets the crew release the electro-mechanical brake by hand for a safe, controlled, speed-limited descent to the ground. No one is left stranded.
No. Only FAS-authorised 3/8 in. (9.5 mm) 5×K19S-SFC steel wire rope, minimum breaking force 16,200 lbf. Using non-authorised rope voids safe operation. We supply certified rope with the hoist.
